Fitt's Law

Mix

mix.com

This company has brilliantly describes Fitt's law. It is very clear what the company wants you to do. They want you to get the Mix app. The company made the button to get the app huge and close to where the user would rest there thumb so that it is easy to press.

Repetition

MSN - Microsoft

msn.com

MSN has a webpage that very clearly uses the prinicple of repetition. Their consistency with how theyorganize and present their articles provides organized flow through the articles. It also helps them keep you scrolling to the next article, which keeps you on their site.

White Space

Xbox - Microsoft

xbox.com

Even before I was studying web development, I always thought Xbox had a great looking site. In this screenshot we can see some great use of white space. The white space helps the whole page feel really clean and organized. It makes it feel like a modern website. As a bonus, this screenshot presents some great contrast between the green icons and the stark white background.
