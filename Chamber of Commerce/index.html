Ogden Chamber of Commerce

Founded in 1887, the Ogden Chamber of Commerce is a nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing business and community prosperity in northern Utah. Based in Ogden, the Chamber represents 775+ organizations in 15 cities and several unincorporated areas in Weber County.
