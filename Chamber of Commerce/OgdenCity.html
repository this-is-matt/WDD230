Ogden City

The city of Ogden was founded in 1846 by trapper Miles Goodyear. The city is named after another trapper names Peter Skene Ogden. We are proud of the history that we have in the Railroad, trapping, and industrial industries.
Until the early 1980's we claimed 2nd place in terms or population in Utah. Now, we still have a strong population that is still seing steady growth. In 2013 we were listed as no. 16 on Forbes' list of Best Places for Business and Careers.

Ogden has seen varied growth throughout it's long history. We have begun to see slower growth level throughout the last few decades and this is due mainly to expanded growth in other parts of the state that are close to the capital. We have many programs to help bolster the growth levels included new policies that have been passed to allow for more affordable housing and revitalization programs to give areas of our grand city a facelift and some curb appeal.
