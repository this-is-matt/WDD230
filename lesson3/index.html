Site Name & Domain

The name of the site is going to be "Southeast Idaho Regional Report"

Southeastidaho.com

The chosen domain is a simplified version of the sites actual name to promote ease of use and so that users can easily remember and find the site. This simple domain name encourages better SEO and usability. The domain was found through godaddy.com and they consider it a premium domain. Click on the webiste to see that it is available.

Logo

I chose a mountain logo because the region has a lot of mountains, especially close to Fish Haven and oda Springs.

Purpose

The prupose of this site is to provide weather and other important information for visitors of the southeast region. The page will include information on local cities, events, and the weather for the area.
