Target Audience

The target audience for this site is anyone who want sot visit the area or anyone who lives in the area and want a source for local news, events, and weather.

Personas

Matt is a 30 year old Utah resident who loves to fish. He wants to go to Fish Haven to spend a weekend fishing and enjoying the area. He is worried about dressing appropriately for his trip. He is also unsure of what else he can do in the area if he gets tired of fishing. He needs a webiste to give him all the information needed for the region so he an best utilize his trip.

Glen is a a lon time resident of Caribou county. He lives far away from the nearest town, Soda Springs. He wants to be able to stay in touch with the town and the people he knows who live in that area. because he lives far away from Soda Springs it is important that when he drives into town that the weather is good. He needs a website that can do both of the things for him.

Scenarios

It is the Wednesday before Matt's big trip to Fish Haven and he wants to know what the weather is like in the area and he also wants to know if their are any current events in the area that he should be aware of. Because of these desires he checks this website to find all the information.

Matt has traveled to the region and has spent the night in his trailer. He is unsure of the weather and doesn't want to go outside dressed poorly and embarrass himself in front of his fisher buddies. He decides to check the local website to see what the weather is like before he leaves.

Glen wants to go into town to get some groceries but he is worried about the weather. He decides to check the regional webiste to see what the weather forecast is.

Glen heard that there was a new mayor elected in Soda Springs. His friend was running for the seat. He checks the regional website to see if there is any information about the election.

Glen's wife has a flat tire out in Prston. He wants to make sure he can get there to help her fix the tire so he checks the local website for an updated weather report.
